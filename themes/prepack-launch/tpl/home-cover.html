PRE.PACKED

A prepacked fork of Pico -- a stupidly simple and blazing fast flat flie CMS, that just got a lot easier...

Pico is simple and easy to use flat file content management written by Gilbert Pellegrom the founder of Dev Studios the guys that created the famous Nivo Slider. PrePack is a fork of Pico that we modified and packed with some of our favorite plugins to make it simple and easy for us to build and deploy microsites, prototypes and apps. This is a preview release read about the Features here, read our quick start guide or fork it here on Github.
